Overview

Client resident in Houston, USA, needed a 4-bedroom fully detached duplex with BQ and swimming pool on 600 m² plot in Lekki Phase 1. Flying Flourish provided Premium Package end-to-end advisory from March 2023 to February 2024.

  • Zero safety incidents (zero LTI)

Challenges & Solutions

Rainy season delay risk
We scheduled foundation and slab works to finish before May rains. Overlap of block-work and roofing teams saved 5 days.
Material price spike (cement ₦4 200 → ₦5 200)
Merchant lock-price agreement signed 2 weeks after contract award protected 900 bags.
Imported window delays (8 weeks)
Alternative local fabricator pre-approved; same spec, 4 weeks lead time. Cost difference refunded to client.
Swimming pool leakage test failure
Additional waterproof membrane layer applied at contractor’s cost under defect clause.

Lessons Learned

  1. Early merchant lock-price is non-negotiable in volatile markets.
  2. Roofing specialist should be on site before block-work reaches 2.4 m to reduce scaffold downtime.
  3. Imported finishes should be ordered at design stage; alternates pre-approved to avoid 8-week delays.
  4. Swimming pool must be filled and tested 6 weeks before hand-over to allow leak rectification without delaying main house.
  5. Shared Google Drive with real-time photos keeps diaspora spouses engaged and reduces decision lag.
