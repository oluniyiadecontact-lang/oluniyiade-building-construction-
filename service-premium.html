Step-by-Step Workflow

  1. 1. Discovery & On-boarding

    60-minute kick-off call to define scope, budget ceiling and success metrics. We sign an agreement and activate your Notion dashboard.

  2. 2. Due-Diligence Deep Dive

    Full title search at Land Registry, GIS coordinate verification, excision gazette confirmation, soil investigation quotes, neighbourhood growth-rate analysis.

  3. 3. Design Optimization

    We peer-review architectural and structural drawings for cost efficiency, constructability and code compliance. Value-engineering target: 8–12% savings.

  4. 4. Procurement & Lock-Pricing

    We negotiate 6-month fixed prices with Dangote, Lafarge, African Steel and other major merchants. A purchase-order schedule aligns deliveries with cash-flow.

  5. 5. Contractor Selection

    Invitation to tender sent to 5 pre-qualified contractors per trade. Evaluation covers price, programme, past projects, insurance, HSE record and financial stability. We present a scoring matrix and recommend the best-value bidder—not necessarily the cheapest.

  6. 6. Contract Signing & Setup

    We draft a simple, fair contract using Joint Building Contracts Committee (JBCC) lite template. Milestones, retention (5%) and defect-liability period (6 months) are clearly stated.

  7. 7. Construction Supervision

    Weekly drone fly-through (4K), daily WhatsApp photos, and a written report every Friday. Milestone inspections (foundation, slab, lintel, roofing, finishing) come with a punch-list signed by all parties.

  8. 8. Cost Control

    Every invoice is checked against the BOQ and stage completion. Variations above ₦50 000 require your written approval. The live dashboard shows running totals vs budget in real time.

  9. 9. Snagging & Hand-over

    At practical completion we conduct a joint snagging walk-through, compile a defect list and monitor rectification. Final hand-over pack includes as-built photos, spare keys, warranties and a maintenance manual.

  10. 10. Post-Hand-over Support

    90-day warranty monitoring: any defect reported is chased until closed. We also provide a 12-month maintenance calendar (when to paint, service septic tank, inspect roof gutters).

Case Study – The Balogun Family (5-Bedroom Duplex, Lekki)

Clients resident in Dallas, USA, signed Premium Package December 2023. Project scope: 5-bed fully detached duplex with BQ and swimming pool on 650 m².

  • Value-engineering saved $37 400: switched from imported marble to locally polished concrete floors; redesigned roof truss to use locally available 2″×3″ hardwood instead of imported pine.
  • Merchant lock-pricing protected against 14% inflation in cement and steel between Q1 and Q2 2024.
  • Weekly drone videos enabled client to present progress to their mortgage bank in the US, unlocking a $70 000 refinance cushion.
  • Project completed 3 weeks ahead of schedule due to overlapping trades managed by our on-site clerk.
  • Final account was 2.8% under budget thanks to real-time cost tracking and variation controls.
