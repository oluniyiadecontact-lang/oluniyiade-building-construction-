Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our construction services

We handle a wide range of construction projects including residential buildings (duplexes, bungalows, apartments), commercial properties (office buildings, shopping centers, hotels), and infrastructure development (roads, bridges, utilities) across Nigeria.

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard residential bungalow typically takes 4-6 months, while larger commercial projects may take 12-18 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Yes, we manage all necessary building permits, approvals, and documentation required by local authorities. Our team has extensive experience with Nigerian building regulations and can expedite the approval process.

We primarily serve Lagos, Abuja, and Ibadan, but we can undertake projects across Nigeria. Our team is equipped to handle projects in various locations with the same level of quality and professionalism.

We maintain strict quality control through regular inspections, use of premium materials, experienced craftsmen, and adherence to international building standards. We also provide warranties on our work.

Our payment structure is milestone-based. Typically, we require 30% upfront, then payments at key construction stages (foundation, roofing, finishing). This ensures transparency and aligns payments with progress.

While we don't provide direct financing, we work with several financial institutions and can connect you with mortgage providers and construction loan specialists to help fund your project.

Yes, we have partnerships with certified architects and can provide complete design-build services. This integrated approach ensures seamless coordination between design and construction phases.

Our 10+ years of experience, 98% client satisfaction rate, commitment to sustainable practices, transparent pricing, and comprehensive project management from conception to completion set us apart.

Simply contact us via phone, email, or our contact form. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your requirements, assess your site, and provide a detailed proposal and timeline for your project.
