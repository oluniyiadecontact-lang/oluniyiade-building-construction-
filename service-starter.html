Step-by-Step Breakdown

  1. 1. Book & Pay

    Click the button, choose a date and pay. You receive a calendar invite plus payment receipt instantly.

  2. 2. Pre-Call Questionnaire

    A 5-minute Google Form asks for location, budget range, and current documents (survey, deed, excision etc.). This lets us prepare specific advice.

  3. 3. 45-Minute Consultation

    We discuss your answers, screen-share government GIS portals to verify coordinates, and flag red flags like wetland, government acquisition or family gazette.

  4. 4. Cost Estimate

    Using our database of 200+ projects, we give you a per-square-metre cost bracket for either:

    • 3-bedroom bungalow (approx. 110 m²)
    • 4-bedroom duplex (approx. 220 m²)
    Includes foundation type, roofing choice and current material prices locked for 30 days.

  5. 5. Checklist Delivery

    The 30-point PDF lands in your inbox right after the call. It covers title verification, soil test, neighborhood growth rate, and negotiation tactics.

  6. 6. 7-Day Support

    Reply to the same email thread with any question; we answer within 24 hours for one week.

Real-Life Example – Tolani’s Story

Tolani, a 29-year-old UX designer in Lekki, paid a ₦2 million commitment fee for a 600 m² plot in Ibeju-Lekki advertised on Instagram. She booked the Starter Package the next morning.

During the call we discovered the survey plan uploaded by the agent did not match the coordinates on the Lagos State GIS. Further check revealed the area was still under government acquisition awaiting excision. Result: she requested a refund, got back ₦1.8 million (agent deducted “admin fee”), and used our checklist to purchase a verified plot 3 weeks later. Total saving: ₦15 million+ in future litigation.

No. Item Why It Matters

7 Request Land Use Charge receipt Proof that owner pays annual tax—avoids surprise liens
