Roofing Cost 2025: Step-Tile vs Stone-Coated vs Long-Span

Aluminium, stone-coated steel, accessories, truss timber—prices per m².

Quick Answer (per m², July 2025)

  • Long-span aluminium 0.45 mm: ₦4 800 (material + accessories + labour)
  • Step-tile aluminium 0.45 mm: ₦5 600
  • Stone-coated steel: ₦7 200
  • Truss timber (2×3, treated): ₦1 200/m² extra

What the Rate Covers

  1. Roof sheets (aluminium or stone-coated)
  2. Ridge, valley, barge board, screws, sealant
  3. 1.5 mm roof membrane (optional but recommended)
  4. Truss timber treated (2×3 @ 600 mm c/c)
  5. Labour (fabrication + erection)
  6. Scaffolding (≤3 m height)

Pros & Cons

Type Pros Cons
Long-span Alu Cheap, fast install, light Noise during rain, fades
Step-tile Alu Better aesthetics, stiff Higher cost, still noisy
Stone-coated Silent, fire-proof, colour-fast Heavy, highest price

Noise Test (Rain Simulation, dB)

  • Long-span 0.45 mm: 67 dB
  • Step-tile 0.45 mm: 63 dB
  • Stone-coated: 48 dB
  • Ceiling with 50 mm insulation: -8 dB for all

Wind & Corrosion Resistance

  • Aluminium 0.45 mm: OK up to 120 km/h
  • Aluminium 0.50 mm: OK up to 140 km/h
  • Stone-coated: OK up to 160 km/h (heavier)
  • Coastal areas: insist on AZ150 coating (aluminium-zinc) or stone-coated

Money-Saving Tips

  1. Buy aluminium coils direct from factory (Otta or Sagamu) – save ₦200/m².
  2. Use 0.45 mm instead of 0.50 mm for spans ≤1.2 m – save ₦400/m².
  3. Order accessories together—one transport cost.
  4. Reduce truss spacing to 600 mm—saves timber vs 450 mm.
  5. Paint timber truss white—reflects heat, extends life 5 years.
