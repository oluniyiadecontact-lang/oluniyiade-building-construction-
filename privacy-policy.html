Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 September 2025

1. Introduction

Flying Flourish (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates https://flyingflourish.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and international best practices.

2. What We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, location and project details (via contact forms)
  • WhatsApp/phone communications metadata (time-stamp, sender ID) but not message content unless you share it
  • Site usage data (IP, browser, pages visited, time on page) via server logs
  • Newsletter subscription preferences

3. How We Use It

We use your data to:

  • Respond to enquiries and send project proposals
  • Send construction tips and promotional emails (only if you opt-in)
  • Improve website content and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis

  • Consent: When you subscribe to our newsletter or tick marketing opt-in boxes
  • Contract: When you request a quote or purchase a service
  • Legitimate interests: To follow up on enquiries, prevent fraud and improve services

5. Sharing Your Data

We do NOT sell your data. We may share with:

  • Formspree – contact-form processor (US-based, GDPR-compliant)
  • Mailchimp – newsletter platform (US-based, certified under Privacy Shield)
  • WhatsApp Business – messaging (Meta, Ireland)
  • Law-enforcement bodies if required by court order

6. International Transfers

Your data may be stored in Nigeria, USA or Ireland. We ensure adequate protection via Standard Contractual Clauses or vendor certifications.

7. Retention

  • Enquiry emails: 3 years after last contact
  • Newsletter subscribers: until you unsubscribe
  • Server logs: 90 days

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (subject to legal retention)
  • Object to direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Exercise rights by emailing privacy@flyingflourish.com (response within 30 days).

9. Security

We use HTTPS, strong passwords, 2-factor authentication on cloud accounts and regular backups. Payment data is NOT stored on our servers.

10. Cookies

We use only functional cookies (dark-mode preference, form-CSRF token). No tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may break.

11. Third-Party Links

Our site links to Unsplash, Formspree, Mailchimp and WhatsApp. Their privacy policies apply once you leave our site.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will post changes here and update the “Last updated” date. Significant changes will be announced via email or WhatsApp broadcast.
