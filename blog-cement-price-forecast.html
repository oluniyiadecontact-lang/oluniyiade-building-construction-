Cement Price Forecast Q4 2025

Buy now or wait? Honest analysis of Dangote, Lafarge & BUA trends.

Executive Summary

We expect factory cement prices to rise 7–10% between October and December 2025 due to:

  • Higher gas feed-stock costs (dollar-denominated)
  • End-of-year demand spike from estate developers rushing to meet rental season
  • Possible 5% import duty on clinker (still being debated in the National Assembly)

If you can store 200+ bags safely, buy before 30 September. For smaller users, stagger purchases across 6-week windows to average out volatility.

Key Drivers We Monitored

  1. Gas Supply Shortfall: NLNG declared force majeure in May 2025. Plants now buy LNG spot at $12–14/mmbtu vs $8 in Q1.
  2. Fx Rate Volatility: Naira moved ₦1 520–₦1 620/$ in June. Importers of gypsum and paper bags face higher costs.
  3. Seasonal Demand: Historical data shows 12–15% volume spike in Q4 as developers target January lettings.
  4. Clinker Import Duty Proposal: 5% levy would add ~₦150 per bag; still under committee review.
  5. Logistics: Diesel price ₦1 680/litre vs ₦1 450 in January; truckers now charge ₦3.8 m Lagos–Abuja vs ₦3.2 m.

What We’re Doing for Clients

  • Premium Package clients: lock-price contracts signed with Dangote and Lafarge distributors for 6 months (max 1 000 bags).
  • Standard Package: bulk-buy schedule aligned with cash-flow forecast; storage on site using tarpaulin & pallets.
  • Starter Package: advise purchase before 30 Sept if storage available; otherwise stagger across 6-week windows.

Storage Tips (if you buy in bulk)

  1. Stack on wooden pallets 150 mm above ground.
  2. Cover with tarpaulin; leave 300 mm ventilation gap.
  3. Store indoors or under roof after 60 bags to prevent moisture absorption.
  4. Rotate stock—first-in-first-out—to avoid hardening.
  5. Insure against fire and flood (₦0.25 per ₦1 000 cover).

Bottom Line

If you can store ≥200 bags safely, buy before 30 September 2025. Expected saving vs December price: ₦60 000–₦200 000 depending on scenario. For smaller users, buy every 6 weeks to average volatility; avoid panic buying in November.
