Painting Cost per m² in Nigeria (2025)

Emulsion, satin, gloss, texture, epoxy—real prices plus labour.

Quick Answer

  • Basic emulsion (2 coats): ₦650 – ₦850/m² (material + labour)
  • Satin finish: ₦1 100 – ₦1 400/m²
  • Texture paint: ₦2 200 – ₦2 800/m²
  • Epoxy floor paint: ₦3 500 – ₦4 500/m²

What the Rate Covers

  1. Surface preparation (scraping, sanding, filling hairline cracks)
  2. Primer coat (where required)
  3. Two finish coats
  4. Labour
  5. Minor scaffolding (≤3 m height)

Factors That Increase Cost

  • Height >3 m (scaffold rental ₦3k/day)
  • Extensive crack filling (add ₦150/m²)
  • Oil-based gloss on wood (add ₦400/m²)
  • Special colours (deep red, navy) need extra coat (+15% material)
  • Rush job (finish in 2 days) +20% labour

Money-Saving Tips

  1. Buy 20 L pails during Dulux promo (March & Oct) – save 10%.
  2. Combine rooms—painters charge mobilisation once.
  3. Provide water & lunch – saves ₦500 per painter per day.
  4. Move furniture yourself – avoid “handling fee”.
  5. Choose light colours—better coverage, fewer coats.

When to Paint in 2025?

  • Interior: Any month (AC helps drying)
  • Exterior: Jan–Mar & Oct–Nov (lower humidity)
  • Epoxy floor: Dry season only (rain ruins primer)
