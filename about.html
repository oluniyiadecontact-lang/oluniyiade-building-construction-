Pioneering Construction Excellence

For over 25 years, Flying Flourish has been at the forefront of Nigeria's construction industry, transforming skylines and building communities across the nation. Founded by Engineer Oluniyi ADE, our company has established itself as a beacon of excellence, innovation, and reliability in the construction sector.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create structures that not only stand the test of time but also contribute meaningfully to Nigeria's development. From humble beginnings as a small construction firm, we have grown into one of Nigeria's most respected construction companies, with a portfolio spanning residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and client satisfaction. Every project we undertake is executed with meticulous attention to detail, employing the latest construction technologies and methodologies while adhering to international standards and best practices. Our team of skilled professionals brings together decades of collective experience, ensuring that each project is delivered to the highest standards of excellence.

Meet Our CEO

Engineer Oluniyi ADE

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Engineer Oluniyi ADE is a visionary leader with over 5 years of experience in the construction industry. A graduate of Civil Engineering from the University of Ile-Ife (OAU), he holds additional qualifications in Project Management and Construction Technology from renowned institutions in the United Kingdom and United States.

His career began with one of Nigeria's leading construction firms, where he quickly rose through the ranks due to his exceptional technical expertise and leadership qualities. In 1999, driven by a passion to create a construction company that would set new standards of excellence in Nigeria, he founded Flying Flourish.

Under his leadership, Flying Flourish has grown from a small team of dedicated professionals to one of Nigeria's most respected construction companies. His commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainable practices has earned him numerous accolades and recognition within the industry.

Beyond his professional achievements, Engineer ADE is actively involved in various professional bodies, including the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE), Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN), and the International Association of Engineers. He is also passionate about mentoring young engineers and contributing to the development of Nigeria's construction industry.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the growth and development of Flying Flourish

1999

Company Founded

Engineer Oluniyi ADE establishes Flying Flourish Construction Company with a vision to transform Nigeria's construction industry.

2003

First Major Project

Completion of the Victoria Island Office Complex, marking our entry into commercial construction and establishing our reputation for quality.

2008

Expansion & Growth

Expansion into infrastructure projects and residential developments, with operations extending to Abuja and Port Harcourt.

2012

ISO Certification

Achievement of ISO 9001:2008 certification for quality management systems, demonstrating our commitment to international standards.

2016

Technology Integration

Implementation of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and other advanced construction technologies to improve efficiency and quality.

2020

Sustainability Focus

Launch of our green building initiative, focusing on sustainable construction practices and environmental responsibility.

2024

Continued Excellence

Celebrating 25 years of excellence with over 150 completed projects and a reputation as one of Nigeria's leading construction companies.
