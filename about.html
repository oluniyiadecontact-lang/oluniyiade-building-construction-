Leadership

BINUYO OLUNIYI ADEKUNLE   R.BLDR   MNIOB

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

He is a certified chartered Builder with the Council of Registered Builders of Nigeria (CORBON) and also a member of the prestigious Nigeria Institute of Builders (NIOB). A seasoned Project Manager with decades of combined practice experience, he has successfully delivered multi-million-naira projects including the proposed renovation and reconstruction of Agbowo Shopping Complex at the University of Ibadan (valued at ₦4.9 Billion) where he served as lead team member and arrow-head for project valuation.

Other flagship projects under his leadership include the construction of a 7-bedroom duplex on 2 plots in Akobo, Ibadan, and an 8-flat building in Oluyole Estate, Ibadan. He is passionate about translating clients’ visions into drawings and executing them with appropriate construction methodologies, on schedule and within budget.

Professional Qualifications

  • B.Sc. Building, Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife
  • Registered Builder, Council of Registered Builders of Nigeria (CORBON)
  • Member, Nigerian Institute of Building (NIOB)
  • Certified Project Manager with over four decades of combined practice experience

ADENUOYE MOSES

Principal Architect

An experienced Architect with over two decades of practice, he brings clients’ imaginations to life through bespoke designs. He has handled several multi-million-naira projects prior to joining Flying Flourish Design & Build, and continues to lead the architectural vision of the company.

Professional Qualifications

  • B.Sc. Architecture, Federal University of Technology, Akure
  • Registered Architect, Architects Registration Council of Nigeria (ARCON)
  • Member, Nigerian Institute of Architects (NIA)

Our Journey

Key milestones in the growth and development of Flying Flourish

1999

Company Founded

Engineer Oluniyi ADE establishes Flying Flourish Construction Company with a vision to transform Nigeria's construction industry.

2003

First Major Project

Completion of the Victoria Island Office Complex, marking our entry into commercial construction and establishing our reputation for quality.

2008

Expansion & Growth

Expansion into infrastructure projects and residential developments, with operations extending to Abuja and Port Harcourt.

2012

ISO Certification

Achievement of ISO 9001:2008 certification for quality management systems, demonstrating our commitment to international standards.

2016

Technology Integration

Implementation of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and other advanced construction technologies to improve efficiency and quality.

2020

Sustainability Focus

Launch of our green building initiative, focusing on sustainable construction practices and environmental responsibility.

2024

Continued Excellence

Celebrating 25 years of excellence with over 150 completed projects and a reputation as one of Nigeria's leading construction companies.
