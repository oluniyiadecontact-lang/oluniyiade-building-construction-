Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

How do I get a quote for my construction project?

+

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form, calling our main office, or sending an email with your project details. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your requirements and provide a detailed quote within 48 hours.

What types of projects do you handle?

+

We handle a wide range of projects including residential homes, commercial buildings, infrastructure development, renovations, and consultations. No project is too big or too small for our experienced team.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary depending on scope, complexity, and size. A residential home might take 6-12 months, while commercial projects can take 12-24 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide financing options?

+

While we don't provide direct financing, we work with several financial institutions and can help connect you with appropriate financing options for your project. We also offer flexible payment plans.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve all major cities in Nigeria including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano, and surrounding areas. We also undertake projects in other states across the country.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Yes, we are fully licensed by COREN and other relevant regulatory bodies. We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage for all our projects and operations.
