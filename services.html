How We Work

  1. 1. Discovery Call (Free 15 min)

    We listen: location, budget, timeline, fears. We then recommend the most cost-effective package.

  2. 2. On-boarding & Payment

    Invoice sent with bank details (₦ or USD). Payment confirms slot; we issue receipt and service agreement PDF.

  3. 3. Document Collection

    You upload land survey, deed, architectural sketch, or none if still searching. We provide secure Google Drive folder.

  4. 4. Execution

    Checklists, BOQs, vendor lists, site visits or remote videos—delivered on schedule. All communications archived for reference.

  5. 5. Hand-over & Close-out

    Final report contains as-built photos, cost summary, next-step recommendations and a maintenance calendar.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade midway?
Yes. Pay only the prorated difference. Previous deliverables remain valid.
Do you build or just consult?
We are 100% consultants—no hammers, no labor. This keeps our advice unbiased. We can, however, manage your chosen contractors on your behalf.
How soon can we start?
After payment, first deliverable is ready within 48 hours (excluding site visits which are scheduled).
Which states do you cover?
Physical site visits: Lagos, Ogun, Oyo, Ondo, Edo, Delta, Rivers, Abuja. Remote-only advisory: all 36 states & beyond.
What if I disagree with your vendor choice?
We provide three options per trade. You are free to pick any; we simply ensure they meet our vetting criteria.
Is your fee negotiable?
Fixed fees keep things transparent. Discounts apply only to returning clients or multiple projects (3+ units).
